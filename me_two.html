My favorite trip that I took in the last few years was when I did the "Tufts in Talloires" summer program during the summer of 2023. The program allows students to take Tufts classes, taught by Tufts professors, on a Tufts campus, that is located in the French Alps. Each student in the program takes two classes while in France - I took "International Law" and "Sociology of Travel and Tourism". The summer of 2023 was my seventh summer taking summer classes.

My favorite restaurant is Hodgdon Food-on-the-Run, one of the Tufts dining establishments. For my last two years as an undergraduate student at Tufts I lived directly across the street from Hodgdon ("Hodge"), making it very convenient. Furthermore, they accepted the dining plan meal swipes in exchange for a specific cash equivalency, meaning that I didn't have to pay extra to get food from Hodge compared to eating in one of the dining halls. The website for Hodge can be found here.

My favorite food is carrot cake. I love how my family usually has carrot cake, which comes without the frosting, but I also love carrot cake with the standard cream cheese frosting. I prefer to eat frosted carrot cake, and perfer it to be slightly chilled (cooler than room temperature but not straight out of the refrigerator). Its delicious, and because it contains carrots I can pretend that it is a little bit healthy, since carrots are a vegetable.
